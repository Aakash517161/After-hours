After Hours started with a simple observation: Bhairahawa sleeps too early for its thinkers. We built a sanctuary for those who do their best work when the sun is down.

We source 100% organic beans from the high-altitude plantations, roasting them in small batches to preserve the bold notes unique to Nepali soil. No loud music. No bright lights. Just you and the perfect cup.
