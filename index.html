Our Story

A sanctuary for night owls & coffee devotees

Born from a love of late-night conversations and exceptional coffee, After Hours Café is a haven where time slows down and every cup tells a story. We source our beans from small-batch roasters across three continents.

Our baristas are artisans — each drink is crafted with precision and passion. Whether you're burning the midnight oil or seeking a quiet corner to create, After Hours welcomes you into its warm embrace.
